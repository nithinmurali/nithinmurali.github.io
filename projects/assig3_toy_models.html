Specifications:

Coordinate axes:

The x,y,z coordinate axes of the WCS are color-coded as Red, Green, Blue respectively.

The u,v,n coordinate axes of the VCS are color-coded as While, Yellow, Orange respectively.

Functionality:

Press '1' to go to VCS

Press '2' to go to CCS

Press '3' to go to NDCS

Press '4' to go to DCS

Zoom in: Press 'O' //the alphabel O and not zero

Zoom out: Press 'P'

Rotation: The left/right arrows allows the scene to be rotated about the Y-Axis, the up/down arrows about the X-axis and PgUp/PgDn about the Z-axis. The pivot for all these rotations is the centroid of the scene. Pressing the ‘R’ key moves the origin to the centroid of the scene.

Translation: The W,S,A,D,Z,X key allows translation along +ve and -ve Y,X and Z axis respectively.

Output images:

VCS

DCS
